Lion (Panthera leo)

The lion has been an icon for humanity for thousands of years, appearing in cultures across Europe, Asia, and Africa.

  • Scientific name: Panthera leo
  • Average Weight: 150 to 250 kg
  • Average Lifespan: 10 to 14 years
  • Habitat: Savanna and grassland

The lion is a large mammal of the Felidae (cat) family. Today, wild lions live in sub-Saharan Africa and in Asia. Lions are adapted for life in grasslands and mixed areas with trees and grass.

Lions are often called the "king of the beasts". They are used as symbols representing courage. They appear in heraldry more often than any other animal. They are an icon of courage and royalty.
